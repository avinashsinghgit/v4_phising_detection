Protect Yourself from Online Threats

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers use deceptive tactics to steal sensitive information. They often impersonate legitimate entities through emails, websites, or messages to trick individuals into revealing personal data, passwords, or financial details.

How to Recognize Phishing

Stay vigilant and watch out for these signs:

  • Misspelled or suspicious sender email addresses.
  • Urgent requests for personal or financial information.
  • Links that seem unusual or lead to unfamiliar websites.
  • Attachments from unknown sources.

Protect Yourself

Take proactive steps to safeguard your online presence:

  • Keep your software and devices up to date.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Regularly review your accounts for unauthorized activity.
  • Trust your instincts; if something seems off, verify it independently.
